We are happy to announce the launch of the robotics club ViCyber

What are our goals? 🎯

ViCyber's main objective is to improve the abilities of it's members. They'll have the chance to learn programming, circuit design, robot arhitecture and, ultimately, to participate in the "First Tech Challange" competition.
