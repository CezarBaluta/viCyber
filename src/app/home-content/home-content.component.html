Our team

ViCyber is the robotics team of the Tudor Vianu National College of Computer Science. We believe that our high school's reputation as one of the foremost educational institutions in Romania will allow us to attract the talent necessary to sustain a successful robotics team.

Last updated 20/07/2022

Our goals

Our main goal is to participate in the First Tech Challenge. The process of preparing for the competition should allow us to establish the conditions necessary to further foster the growth of our team in the future. In this manner, we aim to establish in our high school not only a tradition for performance in Computer Science, but also in the field of Robotics.
