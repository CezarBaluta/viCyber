Vicyber


We are a passionate robotics team that participates in the annual First Tech Challenge competition.

Who are we?

Vicyber's main goal is the development of the abilities of it's members. They'll have the chance to learn programming, circuit and robot design and ultimately to participate in the First Tech Challange competition
